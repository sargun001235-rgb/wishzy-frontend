🚚 Shipping & Delivery
Shipping is completely FREE on all Wishzy orders, regardless of order value. No minimum order required.
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days across India. Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad) typically receive orders in 3–4 days. Remote areas may take up to 7–10 days.
Yes! We deliver pan-India to all 28 states and 8 union territories. This includes Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities.
📦 Order Tracking
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive an SMS with a tracking link. You can also visit our Track Order page and enter your Order ID and mobile number to check live status.
Your tracking number is usually shared within 24–48 hours of order confirmation. You'll receive it via SMS on your registered mobile number.
🔄 Returns & Exchanges
We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. If you received a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, contact us immediately with a photo and we'll arrange a free pickup and replacement or refund.
To initiate a return: 1) Go to our Contact page or WhatsApp us. 2) Share your Order ID and a photo of the issue. 3) Our team will arrange a free pickup within 48 hours. 4) Replacement or refund processed within 5–7 business days.
Yes! If your product is unused and in original packaging, you can request an exchange within 7 days. Contact our support team and we'll help you choose an alternative.
