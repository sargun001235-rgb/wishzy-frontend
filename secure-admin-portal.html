📖 Admin Guide — How to Use Wishzy Dashboard

➕ How to Add a Product

  1. Click "➕ Add Product" button in top bar or Products panel
  2. Enter the product Title, Sale Price, Original Price, and Category
  3. Add the product image URLs (one per line — from your supplier or Unsplash)
  4. Write a compelling description and add feature bullet points
  5. Select Badge (Hot, New, Trending, Sale) and Stock Status
  6. Click "Save Product" — it's instantly live on your store!

📋 How to Manage Orders

  1. Go to Orders panel to see all placed orders
  2. Each order shows: Customer name, mobile, address, items, and total
  3. Use the Status dropdown to update order status as it progresses
  4. Call customers to confirm COD orders before dispatch
  5. Mark orders as "Delivered" once payment is collected

🔌 Dropshipping Workflow

  1. Find a trending product on AliExpress, Meesho, or IndiaMart
  2. Copy the product title, price, and images
  3. Paste into "Add Product" form in this dashboard
  4. Set your selling price (with margin) — product is instantly live
  5. When an order comes in, place the order with your supplier and ship directly to customer

📱 To Go Live on Real Hosting

  1. Upload all files to a hosting service (Hostinger, Bluehost, cPanel)
  2. Point your domain (e.g. wishzy.in) to the hosting nameservers
  3. For real order management, connect to a backend (Node.js, PHP, Firebase)
  4. Integrate Shiprocket or Delhivery API for automatic shipping
  5. Add Facebook Pixel for ad tracking via Settings panel
