Return Policy

Last updated: July 2025

At Wishzy, we strive to ensure that you have a smooth and satisfactory shopping experience. Our return policy is designed to be fair and transparent.

1. 7-Day Return Window

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. If you wish to return a product, you must initiate the return within 7 days of receiving it.

2. Eligible Reasons for Return

We accept returns for the following reasons:

  • The product delivered is physically damaged or defective.
  • The product received is incorrect or different from what was ordered.
  • Missing parts or accessories as mentioned on the product page.

Note: We generally do not accept returns for change of mind or subjective dissatisfaction unless explicitly stated on the product page.

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return process:

  • Contact us via our Contact page or reach out on WhatsApp.
  • Provide your Order ID and a clear photograph or video showing the defect or issue.
  • Once approved, our team will schedule a free pickup from your address within 48 hours.

4. Condition of Returned Items

To be eligible for a full refund or replacement, the item must be returned in its original condition, with all packaging, tags, manuals, and accessories intact. Returns that are incomplete or further damaged by the user may be rejected.

5. Refunds and Replacements

Once the returned item is picked up and inspected:

  • Replacements: A new item will be dispatched immediately.
  • Refunds: If you prefer a refund, we will initiate a bank transfer or UPI payment to your provided account details. Refunds typically process within 5–7 business days.

6. Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled anytime before they are dispatched. If an order is already out for delivery, you may simply refuse to accept the package at the door since it is Cash on Delivery.

If you have any questions, contact our support team.
