Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Wishzy ("we", "us", or "our") operates this website and is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, contact us, or browse our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name, mobile number, and email address (if provided)
  • Delivery address including pincode, city, and state
  • Order details, purchase history, and product preferences
  • Device information, browser type, and IP address (collected automatically)
  • Messages and communications you send to our support team

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your Cash on Delivery orders
  • Contact you to confirm orders and coordinate delivery
  • Send order status updates and tracking information via SMS
  • Respond to your queries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure the security of our platform

3. Cash on Delivery & Payment Information

Wishzy is a Cash on Delivery (COD) only platform. We do not collect, store, or process any credit/debit card, UPI, or banking information. Payment is made directly to our delivery partner upon receipt of your order.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Logistics and courier partners (to complete your delivery)
  • Service providers who help us operate our website (e.g., hosting)
  • Law enforcement authorities, if required by law

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your cart items, and analyze website traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect certain website features.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: Contact Page

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date.
