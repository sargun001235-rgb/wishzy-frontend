Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Wishzy promises free and fast delivery across India. Read our shipping policy to learn how we process and deliver your orders.

1. Free Shipping

We believe in straightforward pricing. That's why we offer Free Shipping on all orders across India. There are no hidden fees or minimum purchase requirements. The price you see is the price you pay at the door.

2. Cash on Delivery (COD)

Currently, we exclusively operate on a Cash on Delivery model to ensure trust and peace of mind. You only pay when the product is physically handed to you by our delivery partner.

3. Processing Time

All orders are verified and processed within 24 hours of placement. Our team may call you to verify your address before dispatching the order to ensure successful delivery.

4. Estimated Delivery Times

We partner with reliable courier services like Delhivery, Ecom Express, and Shadowfax. Standard delivery times are:

  • Metros & Tier 1 Cities: 3–5 business days.
  • Tier 2 & Tier 3 Cities: 4–7 business days.
  • Remote Locations: Up to 7–10 business days.

Please note that unforeseen circumstances like extreme weather or local holidays might cause slight delays.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an SMS with your tracking link and AWB number. You can also track your shipment directly on our Track Order page.

6. Missed Deliveries

Our courier partners typically make up to 3 delivery attempts. The delivery agent will call you before arriving. If you miss the delivery, they will try again the next business day.

If you have any questions about your delivery, contact our support team.
